# 产品适配矩阵

Use this matrix to translate market evidence into a product decision. Do not fill a field with generic industry assumptions.

| 维度 | 户储 | 工商储 | 大储 | 离网/微网 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 购买价值 | 电费、自发自用、备电 | 需量管理、峰谷套利、保供 | 调峰、弃电治理、辅助服务 | 柴油替代、可靠供电 |
| 关键系统问题 | 单/三相、备电切换、安装便利 | 负荷曲线、并网保护、消防 | 并网代码、PCS/EMS、可融资性 | 黑启动、并/离网切换、PV/柴油协同 |
| Pack 关注点 | 模块化、低压/高压平台、静音 | 柜体、C-rate、扩容、室外防护 | 集装箱/柜、热管理、消防接口、可维护性 | 宽温、耐候、低温充电、远程诊断 |
| 商业门槛 | 价格、品牌、安装商服务 | ROI、质保、响应时间 | 项目业绩、银行可接受性、长期服务 | 可靠性、备件、现场服务 |

For every priority segment, add a product-fit table containing:

`市场/区域 | 场景 | 强制条件 | 常见偏好 | 建议产品包 | 需兼容设备 | 认证/文件 | 目标价格口径 | 服务要求 | 证据链接 | 置信度`.

## Environmental Translation

Translate a verified local condition into a testable requirement:

- high ambient/solar load → cell-temperature margin, HVAC/derating study, UV-resistant enclosure;
- cold climate → charge-temperature control, heating strategy, usable-energy derating;
- coastal/humid → coating, connector/enclosure corrosion design, salt-mist evidence;
- dust/sand → IP, filter maintenance interval, HVAC and connector protection;
- weak grids/islands/mines → grid-forming, black start, diesel/PV integration and remote O&M;
- seismic/fire-prone area → mounting, fire separation, emergency interface, local code confirmation.

These are engineering hypotheses until the relevant standard, customer specification, or project requirement verifies them.
