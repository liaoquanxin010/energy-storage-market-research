## 深度选择

### quick_scan

Use for screening. Establish all 14 files, but focus on decision-critical facts. Include at least: 10 high/medium-confidence sources; one regulator/grid source; two local channel observations; three core competitors; two customer-pain-point evidence items; and an explicit validation plan.

### deep_research

Use before a distributor agreement, country launch, or bid strategy. Expand to: 25+ sources; 8+ active competitors; 8+ named channel/market participants; 10+ local SKU/price observations; 5+ pain-point evidence items; regional segmentation; and a product-fit and financial-risk review.

## Decision Matrix

Score each relevant segment from 1 (weak) to 5 (strong), explain each score, and do not calculate a false precision total when evidence is weak.

| Dimension | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Demand trigger | Electricity cost, reliability, curtailment, solar self-consumption, diesel displacement, or grid-service economics |
| Addressable fit | Compatibility with the manufacturer's pack, inverter ecosystem, certificates, and ability to localize |
| Channel access | Reachability and quality of distributors, EPCs, developers, installers, and end users |
| Economics | Price band, expected gross margin, freight/duty, warranty reserve, and payment terms |
| Execution risk | Compliance, permitting, grid connection, FX, political, service, and credit risks; reverse score |
| Evidence confidence | Whether the underlying data is current, local, and independently corroborated |

Recommend `enter now`, `validate first`, `partner-led only`, or `defer`. A recommendation must name the required product package, preferred route to market, and first proof point.

## Pack-Factory Product Brief

For each priority scenario, specify only evidence-supported requirements:

1. System: AC/DC coupling, nominal voltage, phase/frequency, inverter compatibility, grid-forming/backup need, PV integration.
2. Battery: chemistry, usable energy, voltage platform, C-rate, expansion, cycle-life basis, BMS/EMS protocol, remote monitoring.
3. Environment: ambient/altitude, charge/discharge derating, IP, UV, dust, salt mist, humidity, seismic, indoor/outdoor and acoustic limits.
4. Safety/compliance: cell/system standards, thermal-event mitigation, fire interface, transport, recycling/EPR, labelling, language, traceability.
5. Delivery/service: packaging, dangerous-goods route, container loading, local spare parts, RMA process, warranty and installer training.

State `当地强制`, `项目/客户常见`, or `待验证` beside every requirement.

## Quality Gate

Before completion, verify:

- The market conclusion is traceable to evidence and includes a disconfirming risk.
- Dates, units, currency, AC/DC basis, tax/shipping inclusion, and installed/pipeline status are explicit.
- A regulatory claim comes from a regulator, standards body, or an identified local legal source; otherwise mark it pending.
- Competitor, distributor, project, and price claims have a local visibility source.
- Product suggestions distinguish a mandatory requirement from a sales preference.
- No local fact is carried over from another country without direct evidence.
